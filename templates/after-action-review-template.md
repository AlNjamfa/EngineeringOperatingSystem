# After Action Review Template

## 1. Purpose

This template converts completed work into permanent engineering improvement.

The goal is not to judge success or failure.

The goal is to understand what happened, what was learned, and what should improve in the Engineering Operating System.

Every meaningful task should produce a lesson.

---

## 2. When To Use

Use this template after:

* Completing a Jira ticket
* Finishing a DevOps lab
* Resolving an incident
* Debugging a difficult issue
* Building automation
* Changing infrastructure
* Optimizing cloud cost
* Completing a project milestone
* Making an important engineering decision

---

## 3. Instructions

Fill this out after the work is complete.

Be honest.

Focus on learning, not blame.

The goal is to improve future decisions.
